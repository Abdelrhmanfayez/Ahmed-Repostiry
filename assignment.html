    • About NU

    Nile University (NU) is a national, entrepreneurial, research-based, and pioneering university that falls within the ahleya category of universitiesbr in Egypt. It is structured to lead innovation and achieve excellence in the four dimensions that resemble the mission of 4th generation universities: Learning - Research - Innovation and Entrepreneurship - Community Impact. Our unique business and technology-based programs, one-of-a-kind research centers, and openness to innovation and entrepreneurship are designed to address critical areas of vital importance to the economic growth and prosperity of the people of Egypt and the region and to engage in cutting-edge applied research.
